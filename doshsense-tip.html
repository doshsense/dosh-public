Today's Money Insight
Budgets Don't Restrict You - They Free You

Most people resist budgets because they associate them with restriction. The opposite is true. A budget is permission - a clear signal of exactly how much you can spend without guilt or second-guessing.

The person without a budget isn't more free. They're in permanent uncertainty - every purchase comes with a background question they can't answer. A budget answers it in advance. The freedom isn't in having no limits. It's in knowing exactly where yours are.

💡 Try It in Dosh
Open Dosh and look at your Everyday and Lifestyle Flex totals. Do you have a number in mind for what each should be this month? If not - set one now. That number is your permission slip. Spend up to it freely.
