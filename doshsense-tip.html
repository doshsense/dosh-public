Net Worth - The One Number That Tells the Full Story

Net worth is everything you own minus everything you owe. It's the single most complete picture of your financial position - far more telling than your income or what's in your current account right now.

Most people have never calculated it. Knowing it gives you a baseline. Tracking it over time tells you whether your decisions are actually moving you forward. A rising net worth - even slowly - means the gap is widening in your favour. Calculate it once. Check it every six months.
