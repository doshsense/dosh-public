Today's Money Insight
Diversification - Don't Put All Your Eggs in One Basket

Diversification spreads money across different assets so a problem in one area doesn't destroy everything. It's one of the few genuinely free lunches in finance - reducing risk without necessarily reducing return.

This applies beyond investing. A single income source is a concentration risk. Everything depending on one thing going right is a concentration risk. Build redundancy into your financial life wherever you can.

💡 Try It in Dosh
Look at your income sources in Dosh. Is everything coming from one place? And in your Recurring Bills - are your savings going to one account or spread across a few? Single points of failure are worth knowing about before they become a problem.
