50 Days - The Compound Effect of Showing Up

Fifty days of financial awareness is not a small thing. Most people never build a consistent relationship with their money at all. Showing up every day - checking in, reading the lesson, looking at your numbers - puts you in rare company.

The compound effect applies to habits just as much as money. Each day made the next slightly easier. The knowledge, the awareness, the habits - all building quietly in the background. The person who checks in on their money every morning is a fundamentally different financial actor. That's you now. Keep going.
