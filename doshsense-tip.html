The Hidden Value of a Simple Spending Audit

A spending audit is a deliberate review of where your money went over a set period. Most people find one or two surprises every time - a forgotten subscription, a category that crept up quietly, a pattern they didn't realise was a pattern.

Where your money goes is where your priorities actually are - regardless of where you think they are. A monthly audit takes twenty minutes and consistently produces more useful financial insight than any budgeting technique. It's the closest thing to a free financial health check.

💡 Try It in DoshSense
Open DoshSense and do a quick audit of last month - Recurring Bills, Everyday Flex, and Lifestyle Flex. One number in each category. Any surprises? Anything higher than you expected? That surprise is the audit doing its job. Note it. Use it.
