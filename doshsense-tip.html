Today's Money Insight
The Language of Money - Why How You Talk About It Matters

"I'm terrible with money" becomes a self-fulfilling identity. "I can't afford that" shuts down creative thinking. The words you use around money reveal your beliefs - and those beliefs drive your behaviour.

Swapping "I can't afford that" for "that's not a priority right now" keeps agency with you. Replacing "I'm bad with money" with "I'm learning to manage money better" opens a door. The story you tell yourself about money is one of the most influential financial decisions you make.

💡 Try It in DoshSense
When you open DoshSense today - notice the first thought you have when you see your numbers. Is it anxious? Neutral? Curious? That internal reaction is your money story in real time. You don't have to change it today - just notice it. Awareness is where change starts.
