Today's Money Insight
Contentment vs Complacency - Knowing When Enough Is Enough

Consumer culture is built on the idea that more is always better. But research consistently shows that beyond a certain threshold, more money produces diminishing returns on life satisfaction. The goalpost keeps moving - unless you decide where it is.

Contentment is not complacency. It's clarity about what actually matters - and the freedom that comes from not needing more than that. Define what enough looks like for you before the world defines it for you.

💡 Try It in DoshSense
Open DoshSense and look at your Lifestyle Flex this month. Is there a number that would feel like enough - not too much, not too tight? Write it down. That number is your target, not a limit. Spending below it with intention feels very different from spending below it by accident.
