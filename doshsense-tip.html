The Difference Between a Want and a Need - Revisited

The simple needs vs wants framework breaks down in real life. Is a smartphone a need or a want? For most working adults, it's both. A better framework is value versus cost - does this expenditure return more to your life than it costs?

That question works for everything - groceries, gym memberships, holidays. It doesn't eliminate trade-offs, but it replaces a binary that rarely fits with a question that always applies.
