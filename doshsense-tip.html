Financial Margin - Why Breathing Room Changes Everything

Financial margin is the gap between what you earn and what you spend. Most people try to close that gap rather than protect it. Living right up to your income leaves no room for opportunity, no buffer for the unexpected, no space for considered decisions.

Margin is what lets you say yes to things that matter without it becoming a crisis. It separates people who feel in control of their money from people who feel controlled by it. Building it doesn't require a large income - just spending less than you earn and resisting the pull to fill every gap as soon as it appears.
