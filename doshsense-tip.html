Spending Triggers - Know What Sets You Off

Most overspending isn't random. It follows patterns tied to emotional states - stress, boredom, celebration, anxiety. Understanding your personal spending triggers is one of the most underrated financial skills there is.

Scrolling when bored. Shopping after a stressful day. Spending freely on payday as a reward. None of these are moral failures - they're patterns. And patterns can be interrupted once you see them.
