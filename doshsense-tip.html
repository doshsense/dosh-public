Subscription Creep - The Budget Leak That Builds in the Dark

Subscription creep is what happens when small recurring charges accumulate unnoticed. A streaming service here, a software trial there, an annual fee that slips past quietly. Together they can easily reach $200 to $400 a month - on things you barely use or have completely forgotten about.

The fix is simple: audit every recurring charge, cancel anything you wouldn't actively sign up for again today, and review the list every six months. Twenty minutes of attention can free up meaningful money every month going forward.
