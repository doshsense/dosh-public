Herd Mentality - Why Everyone Doing It Is Not a Good Reason

Herd mentality means following the crowd - buying what everyone's buying, selling when everyone panics. It feels safe because there's comfort in numbers. But markets are most dangerous precisely when everyone agrees.

The best opportunities appear when most people are fearful. The worst appear when everyone is excited. Make decisions based on your own goals and timeline - not on what everyone else is doing with theirs.

💡 Try It in DoshSense
Look at your Lifestyle Flex in DoshSense this month. Is there anything you bought because others were buying it or it was trending? Not to judge - just to notice. Awareness of the pattern is the first step to spending based on your values, not the crowd's.
