The Mental Cost of Debt - It's Not Just Financial

Debt has two costs. The financial one is visible - interest, monthly commitments, reduced cashflow. The mental one is invisible but often heavier. The background anxiety. The avoidance of opening statements. Feeling behind before the month starts.

Paying down debt is a quality of life decision as much as a financial one. The fastest progress comes from stopping the avoidance. Face it. Name it. Make a plan. The clarity alone reduces the mental load - and a lighter mind makes better decisions.

💡 Try It in DoshSense
Open DoshSense and look at your Recurring Bills. List every debt repayment you can see - name it, note the rate, note the balance if you know it. That five-minute exercise is the first step to making the mental cost lighter.
