The Difference Between Being Cheap and Being Intentional

Cheap means minimising spending regardless of value. Intentional means spending deliberately - generous where it matters, minimal where it doesn't. The goal isn't to spend as little as possible. It's to get the most value from every dollar.

A quality tool that lasts ten years costs less than three cheap ones. A good meal with people you love is worth more than the same amount saved on something meaningless. Know the difference. Be intentional - not just frugal.
