Understanding Your Relationship With Money

Everyone has a relationship with money shaped long before they had any. Childhood experiences - watching parents stress about bills, seeing scarcity or abundance - leave an imprint that drives financial behaviour in adulthood in ways most people never examine.

Overspending to feel secure. Hoarding and feeling anxious about any spending. Avoiding finances because the anxiety is too great. None of these are character flaws - they're learned responses. But they can be examined and changed. Self-awareness here is worth more than any budgeting technique.

💡 Try It in DoshSense
When you open DoshSense today - notice how you feel before you look at the numbers. Dread? Curiosity? Indifference? That feeling is your relationship with money speaking. There's no wrong answer. But noticing it is the beginning of understanding it.
