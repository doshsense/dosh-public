Why Your Financial Situation Is Not Your Fault - But It Is Your Responsibility

Most people inherited their financial starting point. The family, the education, the economic environment - none of that was a choice. Blaming yourself for a difficult starting position is neither fair nor useful.

Responsibility isn't the same as blame. It means accepting that regardless of how you got here, the next decision is yours. That shift - from where I came from to where I'm going - removes the paralysis of regret and replaces it with the energy of agency. You didn't choose your starting line. But you get to choose the pace from here.
