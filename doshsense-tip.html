Today's Money Insight
Your Savings Rate Matters More Than Your Income

Two people earning very different incomes can end up in completely different positions based on one number - the percentage of income they save. A high earner saving 5% will be outpaced by a modest earner saving 20%. Income is your ceiling. Savings rate determines how fast you climb toward it.

Savings rate is far more within your control than income. You can decide today what percentage goes toward your future rather than your present. Even moving from 5% to 10% compounds into a meaningfully different decade.
