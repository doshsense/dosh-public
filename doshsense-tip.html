Today's Money Insight
The Pay Yourself First Principle - Flip the Order of Everything

Most people save what's left after spending. Pay yourself first means reversing that - moving money to savings the moment income arrives, before anything else. What remains is your budget. It works because there is almost never anything left when you save last.

Paying yourself first treats your future self as a bill that must be paid - not a preference that might be funded. Even a small amount changes the psychology entirely. You stop thinking about what you can afford to save and start spending what remains after you've already taken care of what matters most.
