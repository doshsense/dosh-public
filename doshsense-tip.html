Friction - The Hidden Force That Shapes Every Financial Decision

Friction is the invisible resistance between intention and action. Adding friction to bad habits makes them harder. Removing it from good ones makes them easier. This one insight explains more about financial behaviour than almost anything else.

Delete shopping apps. Move savings to a separate account. Set up automatic transfers. Keep DoshSense on your home screen. You don't need more motivation - you need to engineer your environment so the right choices are the path of least resistance.
